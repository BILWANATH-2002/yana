You Are Not Alone

Welcome to the historic city of Aurangabad, formerly known as Khadki, and, most recently, as Ch. Sambhaji Nagar. Steeped in rich heritage and cultural diversity, Aurangabad is a vibrant metropolis in the Indian state of Maharashtra. Our city's name may have evolved over time, but its timeless charm remains constant. Known as the "City of Gates" due to its 52 majestic gateways, it's home to UNESCO World Heritage Sites like the Ajanta and Ellora Caves. We invite you to embrace the spirit of Aurangabad, explore its cultural treasures, and become a part of our dynamic community. Welcome aboard!

Adjusting to life in a new city comes with a wide set of challenges. This is a small effort to make that process smoother for you and your family!
